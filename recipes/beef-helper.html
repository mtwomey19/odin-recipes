Beef Helper

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (1 lb)
  • Quinoa (1 1/4 cups)
  • Spinach (3 handfuls)
  • Peas (1 cup)
  • Carrots (2-3)
  • Red Pepper (1)

Instructions

  1. Boiler 2 1/2 cups of water
  2. Place ground beef on hot skillet
  3. Season ground beef
  4. Begin cooking quinoa
  5. Mash ground beef
  6. Add spinach, peas, carrots, and peppers as beef browns
  7. Add quinoa
  8. Stir and enjoy!
