Scrambled Lunch

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Spinach
  • Olive Oil
  • Spices of Choice

Instructions

  1. Heat up pan and pour a swirl of olive oil
  2. Place 2 handfuls of spinach in hot pan
  3. Crack and whisk eggs in a bowl
  4. Stir spinach
  5. Pour eggs into pan over spinach
  6. Add spices if you wish
  7. Stir / scramble until eggs are cooked to liking
  8. Enjoy the rocket fuel!
