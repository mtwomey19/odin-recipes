Morning Shake

Ingredients

  • Chocolate Protein Powder
  • Almond Milk
  • Chia Seeds
  • Avacodo
  • Ice Cubes

Instructions

  1. Pour 1 1/2 cups of almond milk into blender
  2. Place 1 serving size of protein powder in blender
  3. Add 3 TBSP of chia seeds to blender
  4. Scoop 1 avacado into blender
  5. Place 4-5 ice cubes into blender
  6. Blend and enjoy!
